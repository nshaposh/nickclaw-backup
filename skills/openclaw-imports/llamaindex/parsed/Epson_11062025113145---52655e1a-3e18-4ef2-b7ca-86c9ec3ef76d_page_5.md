October 27, 2025

**To:** **Prospective Proposers**
**From:** **St. Johns County Purchasing Department**
**Subject:** **RFI NO: 2518 Artificial Intelligence (AI) Services & Solutions**

This Addendum #1 is issued for further Proposer's information and is hereby incorporated into the RFI Documents. Each Proposer must incorporate any and all revisions, clarifications, and/or supplemental information provided in all issued addenda with the submitted Proposal. Proposers must submit a copy of each signed addendum with their Proposal as provided in the RFQ Documents.

<u>**Questions/Answers:**</u>

1. **What is your maximum budget for an AI solution per year?**
**ANSWER: An annual budget for AI solutions has not yet been determined. Funding will depend on project priorities, available resources, and approval by the Board of County Commissioners**

2. **What AI solution areas (of the ones listed) is the County most interested in?**
**Answer: The County is interested in exploring all AI solutions that improve service delivery, streamline operations, and automate business processes.**

3. **Is there a page count limit for the response?**
**Answer: While there is not a page limit for responses, we request that respondents include links to supplemental information and materials when possible.**

4. **Are there any required certifications, standards, or qualifications vendors must meet to work with the county on AI projects?**
**Answer: Any required certifications, standards, or qualifications for AI vendors will be determined at a later date and will depend on the scope and nature of the specific project. Requirements may include applicable data security, privacy, and ethical AI standards to ensure compliance with County, State, and Federal regulations.**

5. **Are you open to vendor presentations, demos, or follow-up conversations during the RFI process?**
**Answer: The County will consider participating in vendor presentations, demos, and follow-up conversations after the AI RFI concludes, as opportunities for further exploration.**

6. **Are there specific departments where the county would like to deploy AI?**
**Answer: The County is evaluating opportunities to deploy AI solutions across all departments, with implementation priorities to be determined based on operational needs and potential public benefit.**

7. **What AI tools are in place, if any?**
**Answer: The County currently utilizes AI tools that are embedded within existing software applications, including Microsoft 365.**
