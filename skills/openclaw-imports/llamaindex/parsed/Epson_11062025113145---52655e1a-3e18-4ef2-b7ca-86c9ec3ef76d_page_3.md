## **PART II: REQUESTED INFORMATION**

### **A. County's Interest in AI**
St. Johns County ("County") is exploring the applicable uses of Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies across various county functions. The County's interest is to determine how to potentially leverage AI solutions to improve service delivery, streamline operations, and automate simple business processes to increase efficiency and enhance the function of staff. The County seeks information to better understand AI solution capabilities as well as data cleansing/mapping and conversion, integration, and implementation considerations.

### **B. Specific Areas of Interest**
The County is seeking information across a wide range of potential AI solutions, strategies and applications. The specific areas of interest listed below are areas the County is aware of that information is requested. The list is not intended to be exhaustive, and information related to different and/or additional components and considerations for AI solutions is welcome. Respondents are not required to provide information on every item on the list below. We encourage Respondents to provide information on the areas relevant to them.

* AI strategy and readiness assessments
* Legacy data analysis, cleansing, and mapping
* Staff burden for all stages of implementation of AI solution(s)
* Staffing, training, and change management requirements
* AI-powered automation of business processes
* Data conversion, integration, and interoperability
* Workflow optimization
* Workforce productivity and decision-support tools
* Risk assessment tools
* Fraud detection
