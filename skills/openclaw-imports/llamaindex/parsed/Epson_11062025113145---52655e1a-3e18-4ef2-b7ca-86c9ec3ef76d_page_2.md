**RFI NO: 2518 Artificial Intelligence (AI) Services & Solutions**

**<u>PART I: GENERAL INFORMATION</u>**

**A. Purpose**
The purpose of this Request for Information ("RFI") is to obtain information from the artificial intelligence market to aid the County in the development of a future solicitation. This RFI is **NOT** a solicitation for Bids or Proposals and will not be utilized to make any type of award. Respondents must not prepare or submit detailed proposals in response to this RFI. Responses to this RFI will be reviewed for informational purposes only.

Any information collected through this RFI process may, at the County's sole discretion, be used in the development of a future solicitation for proposals. Submitting a response to this RFI does not, in any way, provide any advantage to any Respondent in any future solicitation process. No Respondent to this RFI shall be prohibited from submitting a proposal in response to a future solicitation.

**B. Respondent Responsibility**
Respondents are solely responsible for any and all costs associated with developing and submitting a response to this RFI. Respondents are also solely responsible for any and all costs associated with response to any follow-up questions, and/or interviews or presentations which may be requested by the County. It is expressly understood that no Respondent may seek or claim any award and/or reimbursement from the County for any expenses, costs and/or fees (including attorneys' fees) borne by any Respondent during the entire RFI process. Such expenses, costs, and/or fees (including attorneys' fees) are the sole responsibility of the Respondent.
